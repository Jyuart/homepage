Résumé

I'm the main protagonist and playable character of the Uncharted series. A former professional treasure hunter, a "deep-sea salvage expert and action-pro". Since childhood, my older brother Samuel Drake and I have held the belief that we are descended from the famous English explorer Sir Francis Drake.
